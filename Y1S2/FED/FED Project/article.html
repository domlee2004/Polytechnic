Cool Tre flip at skatepark in Venice Beach

Travis Yewell Clock July 18 2017 12

Had a blast doing some tricks at Venice Beach skatepark, Just wanted to share my experience that the people there are very friendly and encouraging, if you ever need a spot to skate and you are nearby, you have to head there! Also you might see me there every sunday in the afternoon, please come say Hi and maybe we can exchange contacts and tricks as well :D.
