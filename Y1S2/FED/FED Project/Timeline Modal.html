Our Timeline

1998

In a small town in America, KickFlip Studios was brought to life by 3 friends who set out on an adventure to make skateboarding fun and attractive for everyone.

2001

The company was struggling and the 3 friends agreed that a big change was needed for the company to survive.

2004

Instead of being the coaches themselves, the friends using their knowledge and passion for skateboarding, hired coaches that were passionate, dedicated and committed. The company was headed for a new direction.

2010

KickFlip became the best coaching company for skateboarders and it reached its first 2000 customers. The community was growing rapidly and the customers were enjoying the coaching and KickFlip decided to take a gamble and launch it's own merchandise.

2011

The first batch of the merchandise was sold out in days and the friends increased production , quality of material and available designs. Online coaching also became a feature allowing KickFlip to have a global audience.

2022

With over 1 million customers and 2 millions merch sold, KickFlip is the largest brand for skate merch, coaching services while also having a extremely supportive skate community.
